How to Use the Open Road Advisor

This utility maps your driving route, computes milestone arrival offsets, and queries predictive weather forecasts to assess wind impacts and rain risks along your journey.

1. Select Stops: Enter your starting origin, dynamic intermediate stops, and final destination (e.g. "Seattle, WA").

2. Set Ride Parameters: Choose departure time, planned speed (MPH), sampling intervals, and your vehicle profile (Car, Jeep, or Motorcycle).

3. Scan Highway: Click Analyze Open Road Journey to calculate routes, overlay paths on the map, resolve names, and check milestone conditions.
